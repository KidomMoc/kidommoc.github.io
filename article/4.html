A Lesson About Line Break

km, Dec 12, 2017

I've spent a whole day on a strange bug. It seemed that when it came to a \n in a certain document, putc would failed. That's so strange and at last I changed the doc and found the cause in gdb.

The reason is, that doc is from Windows Notepad and its line breaks are `\r\n`. I've worked on GNU/Linux for such a long time that ignoring it. Here I writed down this lesson to prevent a next mistake.

ALWAYS REMENBER WHERE THE DOCUMENT FROM
